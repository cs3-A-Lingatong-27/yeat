yeat's Lyfe EP

Lyfe (stylized as Lyfë) is the sixth extended play by American rapper Yeat. It was released through Geffen Records, Field Trip and Twizzy Rich on September 9, 2022. The EP features a guest appearance from Lil Uzi Vert.
